Xiaoqiang Huang

Professor of Chemistry

Xiaoqiang Huang began his independent career at Nanjing University in 2021, where he is currently a Professor in the School of Chemistry and Chemical Engineering. His research lies at the interface of synthetic biology and synthetic chemistry, with a focus on developing new-to-nature biocatalytic systems through enzyme engineering and visible-light photocatalysis.

Prior to joining Nanjing University, he conducted postdoctoral research with Professor Huimin Zhao at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where he pioneered photoenzymatic radical reactions. He earned his Ph.D. under the supervision of Professor Ning Jiao at Peking University, working on aerobic oxidation reactions.

His work has been recognized by several awards, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China Distinguished Young Scholar Fund and the Jiangsu Provincial Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars. He has published over 20 papers in leading journals such as Nature, Nature Catalysis, and Journal of the American Chemical Society.
